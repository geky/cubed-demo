WebGL not found!

Unfortunatly it appears that your browser does not currently support WebGL

Browsers that support WebGL:

  • Google Chrome version 10 or higher
  • Mozilla Firefox version 4 or higher
  • Safari on OS X 10.7 with the developer option "Enable WebGL" enabled

If you are using one of these browsers but are still having issues with WebGL, it is most likely an issue with your graphics card and you should update your graphics card drivers.
If you're on windows, you may also need the Microsoft DirectX runtime

This post has much more information if you are still having problems.
